About


My name is Morgan Styers, I'm 23 years old and a Charlotte, North Carolina Native. I graduated from Cato Middle College Highschool with an Associates Degree in Art and Art Management. Directly following graduation, I was signed in Los Angeles, California, for modeling and acting. I moved myself and two cats to Koreatown where I lived for 5 years, was published in 6 magazines and completed 4 international commercials and 7 films, one of which I starred in.

In my spare time, I love to do digital art and sculpture, play with my cats and collect sharksteeth, micro-minerals and band t-shirts. My favorite place to be in on the ocean and I absolutely love to sail.
